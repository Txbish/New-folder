Burger

Description

A hamburger, or simply a burger, is a dish consisting of fillings—usually a patty of ground meat, typically beef—placed inside a sliced bun or bread roll.

Ingredients

  • Bun
  • Patty
  • Sauce
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Heat Buns
  2. Cook Patty
  3. Layer Buns and Meat
  4. Add Lettuce
  5. Add Sauce
